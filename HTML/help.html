Placing an Order

1. How can I place an order for Pickup?

Click here for further instructions
You can choose how you would like to receive your order by selecting Delivery or Pickup on the right sidebar. Then choose the items you would like by selecting from the categories on the left sidebar. To put an item into your basket, click on the item's price.
If you want to add or subtract ingredients from the item, and order two of the item with these same changes, click on the name of the item under Your Order on the right sidebar. This will open a page allowing you to customize the item ordered. At the top, you can set the quantity of the item you want. This allows you to customize a pizza how you like it, and order 2 exactly the same, quickly and easily.


2. How can I order more than one of something?

Click here for further instructions
If you would like to order two of the same item without customizing the item, you can click on the item's price twice.







3. I want onions only on one half!

Click here for further instructions
After selecting the item to order, click on the Customize button. If there are onions, or any ingredient you are interested in, on the item already, then you merely need to select which side of the pizza you only want them on, using the checkboxes next to the ingredient's name. If the item doesn't have the ingredient you would like, you can click directly on the left or right checkboxes to add that ingredient just to that side.





4. I like my food prepared in a certain way, how can I tell you?

Click here for further instructions
When you have added an item to your order, click the name of the item on the right. You will now see a screen with details of the ingredients on the item and other ingredients available. If the item allows it, there will be a Your Instructions box at the bottom of the screen for you to give some more information on how you like your order.





5. How do I know when my order is ready?

Click here for further instructions
If you have registered, after you have placed an order you can find out if your delivery is on its way by clicking My Orders from the top tabs.








6. What if I forget something?

If you forgot to order something, you should call the store and tell them your order number, which is given on the last page of checkout. You can quickly and easily add to your order.

Checkout Questions

1. How do I pay?

Accepted payment methods are determined by each store. To pay by cash you may need to be a Registered customer or complete your first online order with a credit card.
Don't forget to tip your driver!

2. I am worried about buying online, is it safe?

Our website is secured using the latest online shopping technology, so however you choose to pay, you can be sure that your personal information is safe.

3. Where will my order go?

Your order will come directly to our store, exactly as if you had ordered it while you were standing here. Your information and details are kept safe with us, and nobody else has them.

Registration Questions

1. Why should I register?

By registering, you can save your address and payment information, as well as view your previous orders, allowing you to place your orders more easily.



2. Can I have more than one delivery address saved?

Click here for further instructions
Yes, you can. You can save as many addresses as you like. Just go to the "My Account" page and use the "Create a new Address" button.
